Enhance the Wire write coordinator with retry logic, exponential backoff, and write-ahead journaling to prevent silent data loss under burst formation load.

Output: Enhanced write-coordinator.cjs with retry, backoff, WAJ, and startup replay. All existing write coordinator tests continue passing plus new retry/WAJ test cases.
