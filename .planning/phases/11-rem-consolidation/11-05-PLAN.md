<objective>

Purpose: This is the core of Phase 11. Full REM (REM-03) is the deep editorial pipeline that runs on session end -- retroactive evaluation, editorial pass, conditioning update, quality evaluation, and fragment promotion/discard. Provisional REM (REM-02 execution) runs the same pipeline but marks results as tentative with abort-and-revert capability per D-03/D-04. The REM consolidator (top-level orchestrator) dispatches trigger events to the correct tier. REM-07 (nothing enters long-term storage without REM) is enforced by making full-rem the only promotion path.

Output: Three components completing the REM pipeline: full-rem.cjs (Tier 3), provisional-rem.cjs (Tier 2), rem-consolidator.cjs (orchestrator).
